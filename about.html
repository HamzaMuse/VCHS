About Us

Mission Statement

We are a local, community-driven, passionate healthcare company. We are committed to making our community better by fighting homelessness and getting our services to those in need. We are a licensed Minnesota Healthcare provider that provides great services to the highest degree possible.

Services We Offer

Housing Stabilization Services

We offer Housing Stabilization Services! Housing Stabilization Services is a Medical Assistance (MA) program that helps people with disabilities and seniors find and keep housing. It is for people who are experiencing homelessness, at risk of homelessness, at risk of institutionalization, or living in an institution or some types of group setting

245D

We offer 254D Services! This includes homemaking, respite, adult companion, personal support, and night supervision. For each service, the recipient selects a trusted worker who best suits his or her needs. The recipient maintains control of how his or her worker(s) provide services by scheduling hours and providing them direction on specific day-to-day support needs.

Personal Care Assistance

We also offer PCA! Personal care assistance (PCA) services provide assistance and support for persons with disabilities, living independently in the community. This includes the elderly and others with special health care needs.PCAs provide services that include helping clients: Maintaining their personal hygiene by assisting them with bathing, dressing, grooming, handling toileting and incontinence care.
